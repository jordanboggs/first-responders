Behavioral Health Crisis Intervention for First Responders

Training & Resources

Welcome!

Behavior Health Crisis Intervention for First Responders is a statewide training curriculum aimed at first responders (including law enforcement, emergency medical services, co-responders and others) that are involved with behavioral health crisis intervention.

The Colorado Office of Behavioral Health (OBH) in partnership with The Rocky Mountain Public Health Training Center (RM-PHTC) has developed this toolkit to address the training needs of first responders who work with people experiencing behavioral health crises, which includes both mental health and/or substance use related emergencies. The aim is to increase knowledge of and access to training and resources across Colorado.
